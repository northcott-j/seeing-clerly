About Us

Our dataset to include over 11,000 schools across the country, users can compare reported crime data between a much larger breadth of schools.

As we mentioned in the "Overview" section, this data is sourced from the U.S. Department of Labor, and is still in the process of being cleaned up. With so many schools, it's very difficult for us to validate the data against the official documents released by the universities. In a perfect world, everything should line up, but we can't guarantee the accuracy of all the data our graph displays just yet.

Interested in how this was built? Check out the gihub repo linked at the bottom of this card! We built this on a MEAN tech stack, utilized mLab for hosting our database, ChartJS's javascript graphing library to visualize our data, and Heroku to host the app itself.
